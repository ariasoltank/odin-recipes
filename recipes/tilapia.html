Tilapia Recipe

Description

Start off with tilapia fillets from Costco. Season the tilapia with salt and pepper on both sides while heating up a frying pan with avocado oil. Once the oil is hot, slowly place the tilapia on the pan until it reaches a golden orange color (~6 minutes). Then, flip the tilapia over and cook for another 6 minutes until golden and crispy on both sides. Enjoy with a side of veggies.

Ingredients

  • tilapia fillets
  • avocado oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Marinate the tilapia fillets with salt and pepper on both sides
  2. Heat up the frying pan with the avocado oil
  3. Cook til golden color and crispiness is achieved on both sides (about 6 minutes each side).
