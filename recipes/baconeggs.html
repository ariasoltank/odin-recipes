Bacon and Eggs Recipe

Description

Spray pan with cooking oil and allow it to heat up for a few minutes. Then, place bacon on pan slowly and allow it to cook to crispy (about 6 minutes each side). After the bacon is ready, take it off the pan and re-spray with oil. Crack two eggs onto the pan and allow them to cook for about 4 minutes until they are ready. Sprinkle salt and pepper on eggs to taste.

Ingredients

  • bacon
  • cooking oil
  • 2 eggs
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Spray oil on pan
  2. Cook bacon on pan until crispy
  3. Cook eggs.
